Meet the Team

🤓 Not all heroes wear capes 🤓

Federico Stillitano

Chief Executive Officer

Fed is a serial entrepreneur with a passion for emerging technologies. He is currently working on improving language conversation exchange on campus through machine learning. His interests include blockchain and AI, and is an avid fan of the HBO hit Silicon Valley.

Jack Hadrill

Chief Technical Officer

Jack is a hardcore tech enthusiast with experience in both software and hardware development. Studying in the school of Electronic, Electrical and Computer Systems Engineering, this ethusiasm has translated to an advanced technical skillset backed by strong business acumen.

Alex Young

Chief Innovation Officer

Alex dreams in startups. As founder of Pentagoing, an independent design and UX consultancy, he is looking to network, learn and collaborate with students from Birmingham and its surrounding areas and help develop the local startup ecosystem.

Jack Kelly

Chief Financial Officer

Jack is an enterprise scholar and final year Accounting and Finance student with an interest in business in emerging markets and has volunteered with social enterprise and charities. A Summer Scholarship at Hong Kong University motivated Jack to learn Mandarin.

Mateusz Andrulewicz

VP of Finance
